NYC Shooting Incidents (2006-2021)
Github

This map shows shooting incidents in New York City for two years side by side.

Move the navigator left and right to compare two years of shooting incidents in NYC. The default is 2006 (left) vs 2021 (right).

Zoom and click on individual points for more info on the incident. Dark red dots represent fatal incidents.

Use the drop down menus below to change the years shown on the map, and the corresponding statistics in the tables below.
